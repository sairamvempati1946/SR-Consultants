Our Journey

From one office in Kolkata to a network of offices pan India, take a look at our fascinating journey in the last five decades

2018

SR launches Independent Reference Checks and Career Transition/Outplacement services to provide comprehensive talent solutions beyond executive search.

2022

SR sells FlexAbility to longstanding partner Alexander Mann Solutions (AMS) to focus exclusively on leadership hiring services and strategic talent advisory.

2023

SR launches Amplify + HR Consulting services, a comprehensive portfolio of HR & Talent Solutions delivered by a curated panel of trusted experts.

2025

Recognized as one of India's Top 25 Safest Places to Work and certified as a Great Place to Work for the 2025-2026 period.
